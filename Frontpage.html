Some newer model Trains


Orient Express La Dolce Vita (Italy)

This luxurious sleeper train, launching in late 2024, will offer elegant overnight journeys
through picturesque Italian landscapes. With a design reminiscent of 1960s glamour,
the train will feature opulent cabins and gourmet dining options.
Routes will connect famous cities like Rome, Venice, and Palermo,
providing travelers with a unique and stylish way to explore Italy.


Vande Bharat Express

The Vande Bharat Express, also known as Train 18, is a semi-high-speed,
fully air-conditioned passenger train service in India.
Developed by the Integral Coach Factory (ICF) in Chennai, it represents
a significant advancement in Indian railway technology.
The Vande Bharat Express boasts a modern, aerodynamic design with self-propelled engines
and features like automatic doors, GPS-based passenger information systems, and onboard catering services.
Launched in early 2019, the Vande Bharat Express aims to enhance the efficiency and quality of rail travel in India.







The Paragon fleet trains

The Paragon fleet trains are a series of intercity passenger trains operated
by Great Western Railway (GWR) in the United Kingdom.
Introduced in 2015, the Paragon trains are designed for longer-distance routes
and provide enhanced comfort and efficiency compared to previous models.
Overall, the Paragon fleet represents a significant upgrade in the UK's intercity train services,
emphasizing passenger comfort and operational efficiency.
